Mainway Supermarket

-Opened in 1954 and ran for 14 years, until it was closed in 1968 after being burned due to the riots. Was located on Center Avenue

-Family owned, started by 4 brothers

-Hill District has been declared a ‘food desert’ ever since, with people having to travel outside the Hill for groceries

Salem's

-When Shop ‘n Save closed in 2019, the lot was purchased by Abdullah Salem, owner of Salem’s, for $1.9 million

-Shop ‘n Save closed down due to maintenance and lack of profitability

-65 employees, all living in the Hill

-Operational, but the whole store (which includes a restaurant) will have a grand opening in April
